Improve visibility of properties in map view

User profile

Leisure and business customers. Couples, Families with children, groups (all users).

The problem

When using the view map, I can’t easily tell the difference between available and sold out properties because I didn’t notice the subtle difference between pins with transparent and red circles. At first I didn’t know what those colors meant until I clicked on one red and then another transparent. In addition, I’m only interested in properties available and want to easily filter them without having to reload the map.

These people are still in the discovery part of their travel planning process.

The job the product is hired for

I want to see where are the available properties in the map.

Hypothesis

I believe that [ by adding a legend that serves both as 1. labels that describes what the color of the pins mean and 2. as filters that allow users to hide sold out hotels ] for [ all users ]
because [ that will make the map more legible and easy to use ]
will lead to [ Users finding their properties faster in the map view ].
I’ll now I was sucessful if [ there is an increase of bookings from people using that feature (quantitative data) ].
This will be good for customers, partners and our business because [ people will find what they are looking for with less clicks ].

Proposal

What's next?

If the hypothesis is validated, keep milking the cows.

If the hypothesis is not validated, iterate or pivot, try implementing a shape or color for the pin that helps with differentiation.

If the results of test are uncertain, Gather insights, observe customers and iterate. Reshape Ideas.
